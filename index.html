About

This project was inspired by my personal love for music and my curiosity about how technology can enhance the music discovery experience. Growing up in East Africa, I was surrounded by diverse musical influences from Afrobeats to Bongo Flava. This rich musical heritage inspired me to create a platform that helps others explore and enjoy new music tailored to their tastes.

This project was developed as a part of my Portfolio Project for Holberton School. You can learn more about the school here.
